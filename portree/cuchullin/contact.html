How to Find Us

Queue Tips

The queue is part of the experience. Here is how to do it right.

Arrive Early

Queue starts forming 15–20 minutes before opening. For lunch, aim for 11:45. For dinner, 17:15. Earlier on weekends and during summer.

Dress for Skye

Skye weather changes by the minute. Bring a waterproof jacket even if the sun is shining when you leave your B&B. You will likely queue outside.

Large Groups

Groups of 6 or more may have a longer wait as we seat smaller parties first when tables become available. Splitting into smaller groups can help, though we cannot guarantee tables together.

Be Patient, Be Kind

Our team works hard to get everyone seated as quickly as possible. A smile and a bit of patience go a long way. The food is worth the wait, we promise.

Bring Cash for a Dram

We have a small but well-chosen selection of Scotch whisky. Ask your server for a recommendation. A wee dram of Talisker — distilled just down the road — is the perfect companion to our seafood.
