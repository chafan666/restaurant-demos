Welcome

A Seafood House on the Edge of Scotland

Sitting on Portree's Somerled Square, Cuchullin is the kind of restaurant that shouldn't exist — an unpretentious, walk-in-only seafood house on a remote Hebridean island, packed every night with locals and travellers who have made the pilgrimage for one thing: the freshest seafood in Scotland.

No website. No reservations. No pretension. Just weather-beaten fishing boats delivering the morning's catch, a tiny kitchen turning out legendary platters, and a dining room that feels like a warm bothy on a stormy night.
