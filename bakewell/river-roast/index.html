A fresh start for a Bakewell favourite

River & Roast opened in October 2024, taking over the much-loved Upstairs Cafe space that had served Bakewell for twenty years. After an eighty-thousand-pound renovation, owner Numan Caglar has created a warm, modern all-day cafe that honours the building's history while bringing something new to Market Street.

We hold a five-star food hygiene rating and serve everything from full English breakfasts and eggs benedict to lasagnas, burgers, jacket potatoes, pastas, and grilled dishes. Our specialty coffee and milkshakes keep the locals coming back, and we are proudly dog-friendly with a dedicated kids' menu.
