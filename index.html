Our Story

Rooted in plants,
grown in Wales

Opened at 15 Clonmel Street in early 2024, Pip & Oat is the first fully plant-powered cafe in North Wales. Every dish, every pour, every crumb is 100% vegan and 100% nut-free, crafted in a kitchen where colour meets conscience.

Awarded Best Cafe in North Wales 2025. Bright, fresh, modern, and proudly ethical. Our smoothie bowls taste like sunshine. Our oat lattes are poured with precision. Our cakes happen to be plant-based, but you would never guess.
