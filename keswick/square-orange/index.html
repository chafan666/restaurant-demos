Welcome to The Squorange

A Keswick Institution Since 1998

Affectionately known as 'The Squorange', we've been a Keswick fixture for over 25 years. Our single hot-stone oven sits behind the bar, firing Roman-style pizzas while you watch. By day: Cumbrian-roasted coffee. By night: candles, cocktails, and live music in our exposed-brick room.

Our Specialties

From the stone oven to your table — every dish made with care

Stone-Baked Pizza

Roman-style thin crust fired in our hot-stone oven. Honey Chorizo, BBQ Pulled Pork, N'duja & Burrata, or four vegan varieties.

European Tapas

Patatas Bravas, Spanish meatballs, Padron peppers, stuffed mozzarella, burrata with figs, and pulled jackfruit nachos — perfect for sharing.

Craft Cocktails

Espresso Martinis are our signature. By day, Cumbrian-roasted specialty coffee. By night, a full cocktail list, wines, and craft beers.
