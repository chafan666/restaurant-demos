How It All Began

Founded in 1998, The Square Orange has been a beloved Keswick fixture for over 25 years. What began as a small coffee shop evolved into something unique — part pizzeria, part tapas bar, part cocktail lounge.

Known affectionately as 'The Squorange' by locals, it's run by a former staff member and her partner with the same passion and quirkiness that made it famous. The name? You'll have to ask when you visit.

At its heart: a single hot-stone oven behind the bar, firing Roman-style pizzas while you watch. By day, Cumbrian-roasted coffee. By night, candles flicker, cocktails shake, and live music fills the exposed-brick room.
