A Keswick Institution

30 Years on Market Square

For over three decades, the same family has stood behind the counter at 5–7 Market Square, serving proper fish and chips to locals and Lake District visitors alike. Upstairs, our oak-beamed restaurant looks out over the square; downstairs, the takeaway counter hums with the crackle of fresh batter and the warm scent of vinegar.

We source wild Atlantic cod, cook in 100% vegetable oil, and use proper Lincolnshire potatoes — the kind that turn golden and floury when fried. No additives. No shortcuts. Just honest food, done right.
