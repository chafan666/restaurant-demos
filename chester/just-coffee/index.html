About

A well-designed living room where you can actually get work done

Just Coffee opened in summer 2024 on Commonhall Street, Chester. Most cafes are not built for actual work — patchy WiFi, uncomfortable chairs, music too loud. So we built the opposite.

A ground-floor cafe serving excellent speciality coffee, with a dedicated first-floor hot-desking space designed for focus. Big windows. Morning light. Comfortable seating. Fast, reliable WiFi. The quiet hum of people getting things done.

Established summer 2024

Not a restaurant. Not a bar. A workspace that happens to serve excellent coffee. Come for the WiFi, stay for the flat white.
— Just Coffee, Chester

Who It Is For

Remote workers, freelancers, and students

You do not need to be a tech founder to use a coworking space. You just need somewhere better than your kitchen table.

Just Coffee is for anyone who wants to work somewhere that feels good. Remote workers tired of the spare room. Freelancers needing a change of scene. Students preparing for exams. Anyone who does their best work with a flat white within reach. No time limits. Buy a coffee and stay as long as you need.
