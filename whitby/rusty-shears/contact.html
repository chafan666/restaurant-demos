How to Find Us

Silver Street is a narrow side street running off Baxtergate, one of Whitby's main shopping streets. Look for the cobbled alleyway between the shops — we are tucked down there.

If you reach the swing bridge over the River Esk, you have gone too far. Turn back and look for the turning on your left (if heading towards the sea).

Getting Here

  • Train: Whitby Station is a 7-minute walk. Trains arrive on the Esk Valley Line from Middlesbrough and the NYMR heritage line from Pickering.
  • Bus: Whitby Bus Station is a 5-minute walk. Arriva and Coastliner services connect to Scarborough, York, and Middlesbrough.
  • Car: The nearest car park is St Hilda's Terrace (YO21 3AE), a 4-minute walk. On-street parking is limited.
