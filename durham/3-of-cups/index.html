Two sisters, one vision

Bernie and Lindsey opened 3 of Cups in February 2025, in a small front room on North Road. The name comes from the tarot deck: the three of cups is the card of friendship, celebration, and coming together. That is what they wanted to build. Not a shop, not a parlour, just a warm room where you can sit with a cup of tea and a deck of cards and feel at home.

Bernie bakes. Lindsey reads. Between them they have created a space that feels less like a business and more like someone's kitchen table, if that table happened to hold a carefully chosen collection of crystals and a very good vegan brownie.

What We Offer

Tea, tarot, and something sweet

Royal Cream Tea

A proper spread: fresh scones with jam and clotted cream, finger sandwiches, and a pot of loose-leaf tea. Vegan option available on request. Served all day, because there is no wrong time for a cream tea.

Signature

Tarot Readings

Lindsey reads the cards with warmth and insight. Not fortune-telling, just a gentle conversation about where you are and what might come next. Sessions from fifteen minutes to a full hour.

Most days, no appointment needed

Vegan Bakes

Bernie's kitchen runs entirely plant-based. Brownies, cakes, and biscuits that happen to be vegan but are really just good bakes. The chocolate and courgette loaf has a quiet following.

Crystals

A small, thoughtful selection chosen by Lindsey. Each piece comes with a handwritten note about its origin and meaning. No claims, no promises, just quiet objects to hold onto.
